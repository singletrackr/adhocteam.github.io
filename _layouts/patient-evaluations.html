The Problem

Hundreds of medical forms stranded in legacy software needed to be incorporated into an online solution for medical examiners, and quickly.

The Solution

Ad Hoc designed and developed CUI (Clinician User Interface), to enable VA examiners to evalute benefit claims, replacing a legacy desktop application with a web-based solution that is never out of date, and can run on any platform with a modern web browser. To ease the burden of developing forms for hundreds of exams, Ad Hoc created a form markup language that's easy to understand so that non-technical analysts could keep the VA exams up to date.
