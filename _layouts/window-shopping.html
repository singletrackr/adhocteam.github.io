Window shop for healthcare.

Ad Hoc delivered Healthcare.gov's first window shopping application, enabling Americans to quickly and easily compare plans without the hassle of creating an account.

Project Members

Greg Gershman, Paul Smith, Danny Chapman

Project Duration

5 months

The Problem

Healthcare.gov needed a way for shoppers to view plans without being caught in the bottleneck of creating an account.

The Solution

Ad Hoc was engaged by the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services to fully redesign, develop and operate the Premium Estimation Tool (https://www.healthcare.gov/see-plans/) and Tax Tools (https://www.healthcare.gov/taxes/tools/silver/) for Open Enrollment 2015 (November 2014 - March 2015). With a 3-person agile development team and less than 5 months of development time, we delivered a responsive-designed solution that served over 400 million pages, the highest traffic portion of HealthCare.gov, with zero downtime, over the course of Open Enrollment 2015.
